# Call for Participation (CFP)

**Workshop title:** Healthcare Beyond Reaction: Harnessing AI and sensing for proactive care — _Beyond Reaction_.

We invite poster submissions that explore the design, evaluation, ethics, and deployment of AI and sensing technologies for proactive health and wellbeing. Submissions should focus on topics such as:

- bridging physical and mental health insights
- personalization and bias mitigation
- sustaining engagement while protecting privacy
- explainability
- evaluation methods for lived contexts
- socio-technical governance and equitable deployment

### Submission format

- Poster/position/work-in-progress/provocative papers submitted as **PDF, ≤ 4 pages**, single-column ACM submission template (references excluded).
- At least **one author must attend in person** to present the poster.

### Review criteria

Submissions will be reviewed for:

- Relevance to workshop topics and potential to stimulate interdisciplinary discussion.
- Clarity and promise of contribution (novelty, insight, or provocative framing).
- Fit with practical, deployable, or ethically-grounded pathways to proactive care.

### Publication

Accepted submissions will be published on the workshop website and archived on **Zenodo** for open access.
